# Thermomix Belboula Soup

Creamy Moroccan semolina soup, lightly sweetened with orange blossom water. Perfect for sohour — keeps you full all day.

> [!INFO] Recipe Info
> **Prep time:** 5 min
> **Cook time:** ~35 min
> **Yield:** 6 portions
> **Best for:** Sohour, breakfast, comfort food

---

## Ingredients

- 160g belboula (cracked wheat/barley semolina)
- 1L milk
- 500ml water
- ½ tsp salt
- 2-3 tbsp sugar (to taste)
- 1-2 tsp orange blossom water (fleur d'oranger)
- 1 small knob of butter (optional)

---

## Instructions

### Step 1: Cook the Belboula

1. Add **belboula** and **water** to Thermomix bowl
2. Place **steaming basket on lid** (instead of measuring cup)
3. Cook: **16 min / 100°C / Speed 1 / Reverse**

### Step 2: Add Milk

1. Add **milk** and **salt**
2. Cook: **16 min / 90°C / Speed 1 / Reverse**

### Step 3: Finish

1. Add **sugar** and **orange blossom water**
2. Mix: **30 sec / Speed 2 / Reverse**
3. Optional: stir in a small knob of **butter** for extra richness

---

## Notes

- Adjust sugar to taste — start with 2 tbsp, add more if needed
- Orange blossom water is key to the authentic Moroccan flavor
- Can be served warm or at room temperature
- Proven sohour winner: held Med full all day with zero hunger
