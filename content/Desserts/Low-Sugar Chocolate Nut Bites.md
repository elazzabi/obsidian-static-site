# Low-Sugar Chocolate Nut Bites (Thermomix)

Rich, fudgy chocolate bites with nuts. Low sugar thanks to 55% dark chocolate doing the heavy lifting.

> [!INFO] Recipe Info
> **Prep time:** 10 min
> **Bake time:** 12-15 min
> **Yield:** 18 pieces
> **Equipment:** Thermomix, silicone mold, baking sheet

---

## Ingredients

- 120g dark chocolate (55% cocoa)
- 60g unsalted butter
- 2 large eggs
- 10g sugar (optional — chocolate provides enough sweetness)
- 45g white flour
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 60g nuts (walnuts, pecans, or hazelnuts)

---

## Instructions

### 1. Preheat and Prep
- Preheat oven to **180°C**
- Lightly grease silicone mold
- Place on firm baking sheet

### 2. Chop the Nuts
- Place 60g nuts in mixing bowl
- Chop **3 sec / speed 5**
- Transfer to small bowl, set aside

### 3. Grate the Chocolate
- Place 120g chocolate (broken into pieces) in mixing bowl
- Grate using **Turbo mode** (works better than speed 8)
- Scrape down sides

### 4. Melt with Butter
- Add 60g butter to chocolate
- Melt **3 min / 50°C / speed 2**

### 5. Mix the Batter
- Add eggs, sugar (if using), flour, and baking powder
- Mix **20 sec / speed 4**

### 6. Fold in Nuts
- Add reserved chopped nuts
- Stir **5 sec / speed 3 / Reverse**

### 7. Fill and Bake
- Spoon batter into 18 cavities (fill 3/4 full)
- Bake **12-15 minutes** (toothpick should come out clean)

### 8. Cool
- Rest in mold **5-10 minutes** to firm up
- Gently invert mold to pop them out

---

## Storage

- **Room temp:** 3-4 days in airtight container
- **Fridge:** 1 week
- **Freezer:** 2-3 months (thaw at room temp)

---

## Notes

- 55% chocolate is sweeter than 70%, so little/no added sugar needed
- **Turbo mode for grating** — Med's adjustment, works perfectly
- First batch: perfect ✅
