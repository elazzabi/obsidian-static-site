# Date Night Tiramisu (Thermomix)

A low-sugar, pregnancy-safe tiramisu with pasteurized eggs. Perfect for two.

> [!INFO] Recipe Info
> **Yield:** 4 small servings (or 2 generous ones)
> **Prep time:** 15 min
> **Chill time:** 3–4 hours
> **Safety:** Eggs are pasteurized at 80°C

---

## Ingredients

### The Cream Mixture
- 125g Cooking Cream (must be 35% fat), extremely cold
- 2 Egg yolks (fresh)
- 30g Sugar (reduced amount)
- 250g Mascarpone cheese (take out of fridge 15 mins before starting)
- ½ tsp Vanilla extract

### The Base
- 12–14 Savoiardi biscuits (Ladyfingers)
- 150g Strong Decaf Coffee (cooled)
- Unsweetened cocoa powder (for dusting)

---

## Instructions

### 1. Whip the Cooking Cream (Careful Step)

1. Make sure your mixing bowl is clean, dry, and cool
2. Insert the **Butterfly Whisk**
3. Add 125g Cooking Cream (35%)
4. Whip on **Speed 3**

> [!WARNING] Critical Warning
> Since this is cooking cream with stabilizers, it will whip very fast (likely 30–50 seconds). Watch through the lid hole constantly. Stop exactly when the sound changes to a low "thud" and the cream looks stiff.

5. Transfer the whipped cream to a bowl and put it in the fridge immediately

### 2. Cook the Yolks (Pasteurization)

1. No need to wash the bowl yet
2. Add 2 egg yolks, 30g sugar, and ½ tsp vanilla
3. Ensure Butterfly Whisk is still in place
4. Cook for **6 minutes / 80°C / Speed 3**

> [!CHECK] Result Check
> The mixture should be pale and foamy. Let it sit in the bowl for about 10 minutes to cool down (stir occasionally) until lukewarm to the touch.

### 3. Combine with Mascarpone

1. Add 250g Mascarpone to the cooled yolk mixture
2. Mix for **20 seconds / Speed 3**
3. Remove the Butterfly Whisk

### 4. The Final Fold

1. Get your whipped cream from the fridge
2. Using a spatula, gently fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture by hand
3. Lift from the bottom and fold over the top

> [!TIP] Don't over-mix
> Do not stir vigorously, or you will lose the fluffiness.

### 5. Assembly

1. Use a small rectangular dish (approx 15x15cm) or individual glasses
2. **Tip for less sugar:** Quickly brush the sugar crust off the biscuits before dipping
3. Dip biscuits briefly in the cooled decaf coffee (don't soak them)
4. Layer: biscuits → cream → biscuits → cream
5. Cover and refrigerate for at least 3 hours

### 6. Serve

1. Just before serving, dust the top with cocoa powder
2. Enjoy your worry-free dessert! 🍰
