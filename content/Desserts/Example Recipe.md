This is a sample recipe to demonstrate the static site generator.

> [!NOTE] Recipe Info
> **Prep time:** 10 min | **Cook time:** 20 min | **Serves:** 4

## Ingredients

- 200g flour

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 180°C
2. Mix dry ingredients
3. Add wet ingredients
4. Bake for 20 minutes
