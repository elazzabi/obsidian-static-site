# Moroccan Chocolate Velvet Cake (Thermomix)
*A moist, Lben-based chocolate cake with stable Chocolate Kiri Frosting.*

**Adapted from:** Red Velvet (No red dye, reduced sugar).
**Key Tweaks:** Lben instead of Buttermilk; Kiri instead of Cream Cheese; 370g sugar total (down from 600g).

## Ingredients

### Phase 1: Sugar Prep
*   **150g** Granulated sugar (Reserved for Frosting)
*   **220g** Granulated sugar (Reserved for Cake)

### Phase 2: The Cake Batter
*   **220g** Sugar (from Phase 1)
*   **300g** Plain flour (All-purpose)
*   **250g** Lben (Fermented milk)
*   **200ml** Vegetable oil (Sunflower or Table oil)
*   **2** Large eggs
*   **20g** Cocoa powder (Unsweetened / Cacao amer)
*   **1 tsp** Baking powder (Levure chimique)
*   **1 tsp** Bicarbonate of soda (Bicarbonate de soude)
*   **1 tsp** White vinegar
*   **1 tsp** Vanilla extract
*   **½ tsp** Salt

### Phase 3: Chocolate Kiri Frosting
*   **150g** Homemade icing sugar (prepared in Step 1)
*   **20–30g** Cocoa powder (to taste)
*   **100g** Butter, softened (room temperature)
*   **14 Squares** Kiri cheese (approx. 250g total), unwrapped
*   **1 tsp** Vanilla extract
*   *Optional:* 1 tsp Milk (only if mixture is too dry)

## Instructions

### 1. Prepare the Sugars (Dry Bowl)
1.  **For Frosting:** Place 150g sugar and 14g cocoa powder in the dry bowl. **Mill 15 sec / Speed 10**. Transfer to a separate bowl and set aside.
2.  **For Cake:** Place 220g sugar in the bowl. **Mill 3 sec / Speed 9**. Leave in the bowl.

### 2. Make the Cake Batter
1.  **Preheat** oven to 170°C. Grease and line two 20cm tins.
2.  Add oil, Lben, eggs, vanilla, and vinegar to the sugar in the bowl. **Mix 30 sec / Speed 4**.
3.  Add flour, 20g cocoa, baking powder, bicarbonate, and salt.
4.  **Mix 20 sec / Speed 5**. Scrape down sides. **Mix again 10 sec / Speed 5**.
5.  **Bake:** Pour into tins. Bake **35–40 mins** or until a skewer comes out clean.
6.  **Cooling:** Let cool in tins for 10 mins. Turn out onto a flat plate or parchment paper (avoid wire racks to prevent breaking). Cool completely.

### 3. Make the Frosting (Clean, Dry Bowl)
1.  **Combine:** Add the reserved cocoa icing sugar, softened butter, Kiri squares, and vanilla.
2.  **Mix:**
    *   *Option A (Butterfly):* If butter is very soft, insert Butterfly. **Whip 30 sec / Speed 3**.
    *   *Option B (Blades - Recommended):* If Kiri is cold/firm, omit Butterfly. **Mix 20 sec / Speed 5** to break it down, then scrape sides and **mix 20 sec / Speed 4** until creamy.
3.  **Check:** If too stiff, add 1 tsp milk. If perfect, use immediately.

### 4. Assembly & Decoration
1.  **Trim:** Slice the rounded "dome" off the cooled cake layers to make them flat.
2.  **Crumble:** Place the trimmings in the clean Thermomix bowl. **Pulse Turbo 0.5 sec** (2-3 times) to create crumbs.
3.  **Frost:** Spread frosting between layers and cover the top/sides.
4.  **Decorate:** Sprinkle the chocolate crumbs over the top or press into the base.

**Storage:** Store in the fridge (due to the Kiri). Remove 30 minutes before serving to soften the frosting.
