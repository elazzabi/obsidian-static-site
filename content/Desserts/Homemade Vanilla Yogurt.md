# Homemade Vanilla Yogurt (Oven Method)

Dead simple yogurt using residual oven heat. No yogurt maker needed.

> [!INFO] Recipe Info
> **Prep time:** 5 min
> **Incubation time:** 6+ hours (overnight ideal)
> **Yield:** ~9 small jars
> **Equipment:** Blender, oven, small glass jars

---

## Ingredients

- 1 liter milk (whole milk works best)
- 2 vanilla yogurts (store-bought, as starter culture)
- 2 tbsp sugar

---

## Instructions

### 1. Preheat Oven
- Set oven to **100°C**
- Let it reach temperature

### 2. Blend
- Add milk, vanilla yogurts, and sugar to blender
- Blend for **2-3 minutes** until fully combined

### 3. Pour
- Pour mixture into small glass jars/containers
- **Do NOT use lids**, leave them open

### 4. Incubate
- Place jars in the hot oven
- Leave for **a few minutes** with oven ON
- **Turn OFF the oven**
- Leave jars inside for **at least 6 hours** (overnight is best)

### 5. Refrigerate
- Once set, put lids on
- Refrigerate and enjoy

---

## Notes

- The residual heat keeps the oven warm enough for cultures to work
- If yogurt doesn't set, try leaving in oven longer or start with hotter oven
- Vanilla yogurt as starter adds flavor + live cultures
- Works great for sohour during Ramadan 🌙
