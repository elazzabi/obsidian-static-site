# Chicken Burrito Bowl

A quick, high-protein bowl using leftover chicken. No cooking required.

## Ingredients
- Leftover chicken (shredded or cubed)
- 1 can kidney beans (drained)
- 1 can sweet corn (drained)
- 1 avocado (cubed)
- 1-2 tomatoes (diced)
- Olive oil
- Salt & pepper
- Lime/lemon juice (optional)

## Instructions
1. Shred or cube the leftover chicken
2. Drain and rinse the beans and corn
3. Dice the tomato and cube the avocado
4. Toss everything together in a bowl
5. Drizzle with olive oil, squeeze of lime/lemon
6. Season with salt and pepper

## Notes
- Perfect for using up leftover pan-seared chicken
- Add rice or quinoa for extra carbs
- Works great with any protein (steak, shrimp, tofu)
- Can add: jalapeños, cilantro, cheese, sour cream
