# Mini Quiches — Batch of 24 (Thermomix)

Easy batch recipe using store-bought crusts and Jaouda Crème Cuisson. One carton = one batch, no milk needed.

> [!INFO] Recipe Info
> **Prep time:** 15 min
> **Cook time:** 20-25 min
> **Yield:** 24 mini quiches
> **Key ingredient:** Jaouda Crème Cuisson 500ml (18% fat)

---

## Ingredients

### The Base & Fillings
- 24 store-bought mini quiche crusts
- Cooked chicken (shredded or diced)
- Cooked minced meat
- Cooked mushrooms
- 150-200g grated cheese (Gruyère, Emmental, or mild cheddar)

### The Custard Batter
- 4 large eggs
- 500ml Jaouda Crème Cuisson (18% M.G.) — entire carton
- ½ tsp salt
- ¼ tsp black pepper
- Generous pinch of ground nutmeg

---

## Instructions

### 1. Preheat & Prep
- Preheat oven to **180°C**
- Arrange all 24 crusts across two large baking trays

### 2. Distribute Fillings
- **Tray 1 (12 crusts):** Chicken + half the mushrooms
- **Tray 2 (12 crusts):** Minced meat + remaining mushrooms
- Sprinkle grated cheese evenly over all 24 crusts

### 3. Mix the Custard (Thermomix)
1. Crack 4 eggs into Thermomix bowl
2. Pour in entire 500ml carton of Jaouda cream
3. Add salt, pepper, and nutmeg
4. **Mix: 30 sec / Speed 4** — until smooth and slightly frothy

### 4. Pour & Bake
- Pour custard into crusts, filling just below the rim
- Bake **20-25 minutes** until:
  - Custard is puffed up
  - Set in the middle
  - Golden brown on top

---

## Notes

- **Why Jaouda works:** At 18% fat, it's light enough to use straight without diluting with milk. One carton = perfect consistency.
- **Filling flexibility:** Use any cooked protein + veggies you have on hand
- **Storage:** Refrigerate up to 3 days. Reheat at 160°C for 10 min or microwave 30 sec.
- **Freezing:** Freeze baked quiches up to 2 months. Reheat from frozen at 180°C for 15-20 min.
