# 2-in-1 Ramadan Dough (Thermomix)

A versatile master dough for mini pizzas AND oregano batbouts/pitas. Perfect for batch-prepping during Ramadan.

> [!INFO] Recipe Info
> **Prep time:** 3 min
> **Knead time:** 3 min
> **Rest time:** 15 min
> **Yield:** ~20-25 pieces total

---

## Ingredients

- 270–280g water (start with 250g, keep the rest ready)
- 1 tbsp instant dry yeast (or 15g fresh yeast)
- 1 tsp sugar
- 250g all-purpose white flour
- 250g whole wheat flour (farine complète)
- 30g olive oil
- 1 tsp salt
- 1–2 tsp dried oregano

---

## Part 1: Thermomix Dough Preparation

### 1. Activate the Yeast
- Add 250g water, yeast, and sugar to the mixing bowl
- Warm for **2 min / 37°C / Speed 2**

### 2. Add Ingredients
- Add 250g white flour, 250g whole wheat flour
- Add the extra 20–30g water (if using whole flour), olive oil, and salt

### 3. Add Flavor & Knead
- Rub the dried oregano firmly between your palms as you drop it in (releases the oils)
- Start **Dough mode (Knead) for 3 min**

> [!TIP] Hydration check
> Look through the lid hole — if it looks too dry, add another tiny splash of water.

### 4. Short Rest
- Transfer dough to an oiled bowl
- Cover with a towel
- Rest for just **15 minutes**

### 5. Shape
- Roll out on a floured surface
- Use a cookie cutter or inverted glass to punch out circles

---

## Part 2: The Oven Split

> [!IMPORTANT] Oven Settings
> **220°C – 240°C**, middle-bottom rack

Decide how many circles you want as pizzas vs batbouts before baking!

---

### Path A: Mini Pizzas (Flat)

> [!WARNING] Crucial Step
> You **MUST** vigorously poke these with a fork all over the center so they don't puff up.

**For the Freezer (Par-baked):**
1. Bake the pricked, plain dough for **4–5 minutes** until dry but pale
2. Cool completely on a wire rack
3. Freeze in zip-top bags

**For Eating Today:**
1. Add sauce, cheese, and toppings to the pricked raw dough
2. Bake for **8–12 minutes** until golden and bubbling

---

### Path B: Oregano Batbouts / Pitas (Puffed)

> [!WARNING] Crucial Step
> Do **NOT** poke these with a fork. Leave the dough completely intact.

**Bake:**
1. Put the plain, unpricked circles in the hot oven
2. Watch them balloon up!
3. Bake for **5–7 minutes** until puffed, set, and slightly golden

**Cool & Store:**
1. Remove and let cool on a wire rack (they might deflate slightly but the pocket remains)
2. Once completely cool, freeze in zip-top bags

**To Serve:**
- Thaw, slice open the pocket
- Stuff with your favorite fillings (tuna, chicken, cheese, etc.)
