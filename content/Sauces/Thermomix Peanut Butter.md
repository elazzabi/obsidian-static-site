# Thermomix Peanut Butter

Pure, preservative-free peanut butter in under 5 minutes. Perfect for quick, protein-packed snacks.

> [!INFO] Recipe Info
> **Prep time:** 5 min
> **Yield:** ~500g (1 large jar)
> **Storage:** 2 weeks room temp, months in fridge

---

## Ingredients

- 500g roasted peanuts (unsalted preferred — you control the sodium)
- 1/2 tsp sea salt (optional, adjust to taste)
- 1–2 tbsp peanut oil or coconut oil (optional — only if you want creamier/runnier texture)

---

## Instructions

### 1. First Grind
- Pour roasted peanuts (and salt, if using) into mixing bowl
- Grind **1 min / Speed 9**

### 2. Scrape
- Scrape down sides and bottom with spatula
- At this point it will look like thick paste or powder

### 3. Blend to Creamy
- Blend **1 min / Speed 6** until oils release and it becomes smooth and creamy

### 4. Optional Extra Creaminess
- If you want it even creamier, add the optional oil
- Blend another **20-30 sec / Speed 6**

### 5. Store
- Transfer to an airtight sterilized glass jar

---

## Storage

- **Room temp:** 2 weeks
- **Fridge:** Several months

---

## Notes

- Unsalted peanuts give you control over sodium
- Natural separation is normal — just stir before use
- The oils release naturally during blending — be patient
