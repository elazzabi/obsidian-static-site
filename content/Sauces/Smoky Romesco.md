# Smoky Romesco (Roasted Red Pepper)
*Rich, savory, and cooked. A perfect "red sauce" alternative.*

**Status:** Pregnancy Safe (Cooked)
**Shelf Life:** 5-7 days in fridge.

## Ingredients
*   **Red Peppers:** 2-3 large fresh peppers (roasted & peeled) OR 1 jar (250g-300g) roasted peppers (drained).
*   **Nuts:** 1/2 cup Almonds (roasted/unsalted) or Walnuts.
*   **Garlic:** 1 clove.
*   **Acidity:** 1 tbsp Sherry Vinegar (or Red Wine Vinegar).
*   **Spice:** 1 tsp Smoked Paprika (crucial for flavor).
*   **Oil:** 1/3 cup Olive Oil.
*   Salt to taste.

## Preparation: Fresh Peppers (The Better Way)
1.  **Roast:** Cut peppers in half, remove seeds. Place skin-side up on a baking sheet.
2.  **Broil/Grill:** Cook on high in the oven until skins are **completely black and blistered** (15-20 mins).
3.  **Steam:** Place hot peppers in a bowl and cover tightly with plastic wrap/plate for 10 mins.
4.  **Peel:** Slide the skins off and discard. Use the soft flesh.

## Thermomix Method
1.  Add **Almonds** and **Garlic**.
    *   **Mill 5 sec / Speed 7**. (Aim for crumbs, not flour).
2.  Add **Peppers**, **Vinegar**, **Paprika**, **Salt**.
    *   **Blend 10 sec / Speed 6**.
3.  Scrape down sides. Add **Olive Oil**.
4.  **Mix 10 sec / Speed 4 / Reverse** (for texture) OR **Speed 8** (for smooth dip).
