# Worry-Free Aioli (Aquafaba Magic)
*The closest texture to real mayo/aioli without a single egg.*

**Status:** Pregnancy Safe (No Raw Eggs)
**Shelf Life:** 5-7 days in fridge.

## Ingredients
*   **Aquafaba:** 80g (Chickpea cooking liquid - see below).
*   **Acid:** 1 tsp Apple Cider Vinegar or Lemon Juice.
*   **Binder:** 1 tsp Mustard (Dijon).
*   **Oil:** 250g Neutral Oil (Grapeseed/Light Olive). *Weigh in a jug first.*
*   **Garlic:** 1 clove (omit for plain Mayo).
*   Salt to taste.

## How to make DIY Aquafaba
*Better than canned liquid.*
1.  **Soak:** Dry chickpeas overnight.
2.  **Cook:** Boil in fresh water (just covering beans by 2cm) until soft.
3.  **Reduce:** Remove beans. Boil the remaining liquid down until it is viscous/slimy like raw egg whites.
4.  **Chill:** MUST be completely cold to whip.

## Thermomix Method
*Bowl must be 100% dry and grease-free.*

1.  Add **Garlic** (if using).
    *   **Chop 3 sec / Speed 7**.
2.  Insert **Butterfly Whisk**.
3.  Add **Aquafaba**, **Vinegar**, **Mustard**, **Salt**.
    *   **Mix 2 min / Speed 4**. (Look for white foam).
4.  Set to **3 min / Speed 4**.
5.  **The Emulsion:** While running, pour **Oil** very slowly onto the mixing bowl lid. Let it trickle through the gap around the measuring cup.
    *   *Listen:* Sound will change to a thick "thwack" as it emulsifies.
