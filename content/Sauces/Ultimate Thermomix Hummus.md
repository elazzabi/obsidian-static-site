# Ultimate Thermomix Hummus (From Soaked Chickpeas)

Silky smooth hummus made from scratch. The ice-cold water is the secret to fluffy texture.

> [!INFO] Recipe Info
> **Prep time:** Overnight soak + 10 min active
> **Cook time:** 45-60 min
> **Yield:** ~600g hummus
> **Freezes:** Yes, up to 4 months

---

## Ingredients

### For Cooking the Chickpeas
- 400-440g soaked chickpeas (drained and rinsed) - 200g before soaking
- 1.5 liters water
- ½ tsp baking soda (for ultra-smooth texture)

### For the Hummus
- 60g tahini
- 40g fresh lemon juice (~1 medium lemon)
- ½–1 tsp dried garlic powder (adjust to taste)
- 50g extra virgin olive oil (plus extra to drizzle)
- 50g ice-cold water (or 2-3 ice cubes — the fluffy secret!)
- 1 tsp salt
- ½ tsp ground cumin

---

## Instructions

### Step 1: Cook the Chickpeas

1. Add **400-440g soaked chickpeas** to Thermomix bowl
2. Pour in **1.5L water** and **½ tsp baking soda**
3. Place simmering basket on lid (instead of measuring cup — lets steam escape)
4. Cook: **45-60 min / 100°C / Reverse / Speed Spoon**

> [!TIP] Doneness Check
> At 45 min, test a chickpea — should squish easily between fingers. If still firm, cook 10-15 min more.

5. Drain well
6. Rinse bowl with cold water to cool it down

### Step 2: Blend the Hummus

1. Add to cooled bowl: **chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic powder, salt, cumin**
2. Blend: **1 min / Speed 5**
3. Scrape down sides
4. Add **ice-cold water** (or ice cubes) and **olive oil**
5. Blend: **1 min / Speed 8**
6. Check consistency — for silkier, blend another **30 sec / Speed 8**

### Step 3: Serve

1. Transfer to serving bowl
2. Create a well in the center
3. Drizzle generously with extra virgin olive oil

---

## Freezing Instructions

- Store in airtight container (leave room for expansion)
- Pour thin layer of olive oil on surface before sealing
- Freezes up to **4 months**
- **To thaw:** Overnight in fridge, then stir vigorously to restore texture

---

## Notes

- Baking soda breaks down skins for smoother texture
- Ice-cold water = fluffy hummus (don't skip!)
- Great with: warm pita, veggies, falafel, or straight off a spoon
